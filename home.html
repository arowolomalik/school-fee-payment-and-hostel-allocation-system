School Fee Payment Process

Our school fee payment system provides a seamless experience for paying tuition online. Parents and guardians can easily log in to their accounts to view outstanding balances and make payments securely. With multiple payment options available, including bank transfers and credit card payments, everyone can find a method that suits their needs. The platform ensures that all transactions are processed promptly, giving users peace of mind. By facilitating timely payments, we help schools maintain their financial health while allowing students to focus on their education without fee-related stress.

×

Hostel Allocation

Our hostel allocation system operates on a first-come, first-served basis, ensuring fairness and transparency. Students can select their preferred accommodation options during the registration process. As soon as a payment is confirmed, their place in the hostel queue is secured. This efficient system helps students plan their living arrangements ahead of time. With a variety of hostels to choose from, students can find accommodations that suit their lifestyle and budget. We strive to create a comfortable living environment that complements the educational experience, making campus life enjoyable and convenient.
